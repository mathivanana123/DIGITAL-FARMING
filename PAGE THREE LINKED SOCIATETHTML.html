GROWING TOGETHER : FARMER WITH THE SCHOLARS

Growing Together: Farmers with Scholars" is a collaborative effort that merges practical farming experience with academic research. Farmers share real-world challenges, while scholars provide scientific solutions and innovative technologies. This partnership leads to practical, research-driven strategies that improve agricultural practices, enhance productivity, and promote sustainability, benefiting both the agricultural sector and society.
